Frequently Asked Questions

Is your service only for Senior Citizens?

While we specialize in Senior Citizen care, we also provide professional nursing for patients of all ages recovering from surgery or long-term illness, strictly on a home-based model.

What areas in Chennai do you cover?

Our headquarters is in Arumbakkam, but we provide home-based services across all major parts of Chennai including Anna Nagar, MMDA, and beyond.

Do you help with medicine and appointments?

Yes, our care takers manage medication timings and accompany patients for doctor and hospital appointments.
